Complex Number Operations

Complex Numbers are numbers of the form z=a+bi, where i is a solution to the equation x^2=-1. The common operations on complex numbers are Re, Im, Arg, Mod, and Conj. For a more detailed description of complex numbers, see this article on Complex numbers.
